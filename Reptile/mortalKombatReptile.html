Reptile

Reptile was a secret character in the first Mortal Kombat game, and was fully playable in the second. At first, he fought using movesfrom both Sub-Zero and Scorpion, but was given his own moveset in the MKII. Reptile has the ability to spit corrosive acid and turn completely invisible. His realm called "Zaterra" was conquered by the Outworld emperor, most of the Zaterrans were enslaved but Reptile was trained into a powerful servant.
