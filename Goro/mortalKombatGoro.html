Goro

Goro was first a mini boss in the original 1995 game and is now a playable character in a select few of the games,including MKX. Goro has four arms and is incredibly muscular, he uses his brute force to his advantage but is also seen using fireballs and bladed gauntlets. Goro participated and won 9 tournaments in Shoa Khan's name, but was defeated by a character named Lui Kang, thus preventing Earthrealm from being conquered.
